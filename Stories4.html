Heartless Ring

Forward unto that heartless ring!

There is no love but of the fight!

Wrap our hands so bones don't break!

Fire in our arms, the China Lakes!


Our blood stains the battered ground,

As our fists smell of burning rubber.

Unconscious bodies lying on the ground

But in the midst of it all, where is my mother?


"Prizefighter"

"Right Hooker

"Mother Fucker"

But I'll never say, "Where is your father?"


We have no lover but that ring

There is no love but in this night,

Us lonely hearts needs to fight,

but those dear will not see us die!


So once more, dance and sing!

Forward unto that lonely ring!
